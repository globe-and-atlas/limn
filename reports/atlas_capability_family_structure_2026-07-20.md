# Limn Atlas — Capability Family Structure

Generated: 2026-07-20

## Scientific purpose

Limn Atlas preserves all 91 documented methods but no longer presents the catalog as 91 independent capabilities or novelty claims. The primary organization is 24 capability families. Each method is classified as a primary method, variant, component feature, established reference, research model, or retired formula.

Family membership means that methods address a shared physical condition or user decision. It does not mean that their outputs are scientifically equivalent, statistically redundant, novel, calibrated, or validated.

## Product incorporation

- **Limn Atlas:** capability-first navigation is the default; domain navigation remains available; research and retired methods have a separate Research view; every selected method displays its family and role.
- **Atlas application:** capability families, formulas, evidence, results, and validation claims are self-contained within the Atlas research catalog.
- **Preprint:** the ecological examples are Atlas-linked methods only. CSRC is a bloom-family variant, LFGVI a landfill component, and TRSI/SWRI research models.
- **Globe & Atlas seeds:** each lead is identified as the primary method in one family. Sibling methods are comparisons or components, not additional novelty claims.

## Remaining scientific test

This restructuring is an honest catalog architecture, not an empirical redundancy result. Output-correlation, hotspot-overlap, recall/false-positive, and failure-mode comparisons are still required before two methods can be scientifically merged or removed.
