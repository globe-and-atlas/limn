Disclaimer & Terms of Use

Screening Purposes Only: Limn is a preliminary environmental screening tool developed by Globe & Atlas. It utilizes raw multi-spectral surface reflectance data from the Copernicus Sentinel-2 satellites and synthetic aperture radar (SAR) from Sentinel-1.

No Regulatory Determination: Spectral responses are non-specific and overlap ordinary caliche, soil, water, and vegetation conditions. The current evaluation found no usable produced-water/caliche operating point at the tested scale. Results do not constitute a definitive regulatory or legal determination of environmental contamination.

Ground-Truth Required: Satellite readings represent surface-level indicators. A definitive assessment of produced water spills, localized blowouts, or legacy caliche degradation requires professional on-site ground-truth investigation and chemical soil analysis.
